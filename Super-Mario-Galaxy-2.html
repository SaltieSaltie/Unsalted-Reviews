Super Mario Galaxy 2

95%

Initial Release Date: MAY 23, 2010
Platforms: Nintendo Wii
Genre: Platform, Adventure
Developer: Nintendo
Publisher: Nintendo

Story

All Mario games have a pretty simple story and this game doesn't stray further from its predecessors; it actually sticks to the same exact formula as its prequel (Super Mario Galaxy). Peach has been captured, again, by Bowser because this giant turtle wants a huge cake. That is all what the story about; short and sweet and Mario games don't need more than that.

Gameplay

The main reason why Mario games don't need a convuluted story is because its gameplay is, dare I say, perfect. The controls are simple but they are very fun and most importantly they are clean and responsive. The whole game is divided in worlds, which are also divided in many galaxies that are built with amazing creativity and mind; you won't find boring galaxies. The power-ups from Mario Galaxy make a return and then some more too! All of the new power-ups are all enjoyable to use, especially the unique ones from the unique galaxies.

Graphics & Artstyle

This game is quite, 2010 old, but it holds up really well! Due to Nintendo not concentrating on the actual graphics but on the 'cosmic' artsyle that dominates the game, it is a spectacle of an artwork. Nintendo has really nailed the 'space aspect' of Super Mario Galaxy and that is what makes it the best Mario game of all time.

Soundtrack

It is jolly good just in like every Nintendo game.

Technicals

It runs really well just like any other Nintendo game!
